About Legacy

What this is

Legacy is a private vault for your memories. A photo, a voice recording, a written story — saved together, so the people who matter can hear them later.

How to use it

Tap the + button to add a new memory. You can include:

  • A photo from your phone or camera
  • A voice recording telling the story out loud
  • A written version of the story
  • The date it happened
  • Who was there
  • Tags for finding it later

You only need one of these. Start with whatever feels easiest.

Where this is going

The memories you save become the foundation for a second layer — an AI trained to tell your stories back in your own voice. Open the Chat tab to see where it's headed. It's still being built.

Your data

Right now everything is stored privately on this device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use the export button (top right of the Memories tab) to download a full backup — photos, audio, and all — at any time.

One small note

You don't have to write a perfect story. Imperfect is more real. Just the way you'd tell it out loud is enough.
